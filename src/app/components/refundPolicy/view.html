TollMinder® Customer User Agreement

In order to provide the greatest convenience to the operator and/or registered owner of a motor vehicle using roadways TollMinder® services (a "customer"), customers may pay their tolls automatically through the use of an TollMinder® account in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. There two types of tolling transponders/passes (referenced herein as "tags") which may be used by TollMinder® customers (a) a simple sticker-type tag and (b) a switchable High Occupancy Vehicle ("GPS") tag. Please note that there are some differences in the rules applicable to use of each type of tag.

TollMinder® ACCOUNT TYPE

Credit Card Account: If you open a credit card TollMinder® account, your credit card will be charged to establish a Toll Account Balance ("TAB"), which is a pre- determined dollar amount, which may as a matter of policy be changed from time to time, and which acts as a pre-charged deposit for the payment of tolls. Each time your TollMinder® account balance falls under $10.00, your credit card will be charged to re-establish the TAB. No TollMinder® account will be permitted to have a negative balance. If you begin using your TollMinder® account more frequently, the TAB may be increased to an amount which equals your average monthly usage in tolls. It is important that you inform the TollMinder® Service Center of any changes to your credit card. You hereby agree to pay a rejected payment fee of $30.00 for each transaction refused by your bank or credit card issuer.

RESPONSIBILITY FOR TOLLS AND OTHER CHARGES

The customer to whom an TollMinder® tag has been issued is responsible for all toll charges, late fees, collection fees, adjudication fees and civil penalties assessed against the account until the account is closed. You shall remain responsible even if a tag is lost or stolen. If your tag is lost or stolen you should immediately notify the TollMinder® Service Center by emailing service@TollMinder.com . In case hacked account, a replacement simple sticker-type tag will be issued, or a replacement switchable GPS tag may be purchased by you. You will not be liable for the applicable tolls, late fees, and/or collection fees if you prove that the vehicle involved was sold to another or that the vehicle was not in your custody or control due to theft prior to the date of the toll. Should a dispute occur over a toll, the TollMinder® Service Center will provide you, at your request, an itemized statement listing the disputed transaction(s).

You may request emailed monthly statements which will be provided free of charge. You may also request monthly statements via U.S. Mail. A monthly service fee of

$2.00 will be deducted from your TAB for mailed monthly statements.

If you fail to keep the required minimum balance in your TollMinder® account, you agree that the TollMinder® Service Center is authorized to deactivate all of the TollMinder® tag(s) assigned to your account. Each use of the TollMinder® tag after deactivation is a toll violation and you will be subject to all applicable tolls, late fees, collection fees, adjudication fees and civil penalties. If your TAB has insufﬁcient funds, you are liable until said fees and civil penalties are paid.

CLOSING AN TollMinder® ACCOUNT · REFUND OF A TAB

If you close your TollMinder® account, then you will receive a refund of the balance remaining in your TAB. This refund will be made to the credit card account used to establish the TollMinder® account, unless the credit card account has been closed. Check-based account customers will receive a refund check which will be issued within thirty (30) days of the date the TollMinder® account is closed.

TollMinder® CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

As an TollMinder® customer you agree to:

1.Notify the TollMinder® Service Center immediately (a) if your credit card has been lost or stolen, (b) the expiration date of the credit card changes, (c) your credit card limit has been reached, (d) the credit card account has been closed or (d) if the account number or issuing bank changes.

2.Notify the TollMinder® Service Center immediately of a changed mailing address and/or phone number.

3.Notify the TollMinder® Service Center immediately of a change to an automobile registration or license plate.

Failure to notify the TollMinder® Service Center of a transfer of a tag to another vehicle, or of any change in your license plate or vehicle registration may result in a $10.00 fee assessed to your TollMinder® account.

The TollMinder® Service Center will charge you tolls at then-current LicensePlateToll® rate for all tolls associated with vehicles not properly registered to your TollMinder® account.
