what is tollminder

TollMinder is the premier link between FasTrak, E-Z Pass and PayByPlate. TollMinder app for Android and iOS incorporates hands free GPS to accurately identify and alert toll users that they are entering a toll road.

how it works:

Once user accepts toll road entrance our turnkey application handles everything else for a seamless interaction and payment. TollMinder logs entrance, exit, time and date. Once complete user pays via cell phone.
